Outlier: the Donatus Awudu Story

Director, Editor, Cinematographer

Outlier was produced by Jimmy Eko-Acquah, and the two of us worked on this project from the very beginning. Jimmy wrote the story, cast the actors, and chose the locations. I arrived in Ghana and shot the whole feature-length movie in 11 days. Our production crew was just three of us, and I developed all of the cinematography on the fly. After production, I edited it into a viable film, and we had some Ghanian editors contribute towards the end as well.

Animal Defender

Director, Editor, Associate Producer

Animal Defender was meant to be a reality TV show starring Stephen Sorrentino. Stephen and I developed the concept of the show which was supposed to be Animal Cops meets Operation Repo. Stephen is a big animal rights guy, and the show would have shown him confronting animal abusers and saving the animals. We produced a sizzle reel to pitch to Animal Planet, but it didn't work out. I assembled a crew with a 0$ budget and directed the production. We were able to pull off a decent sizzle reel.

Adam Mohammed's Story

Producer, Director, Editor

Adam Mohammed is a Ghanaian forest ranger and conservationist. He arrests poachers and illegal loggers when he finds them, and he educates the local people on how to manage their cocoa farms.

Attah Kojo's Story

Producer, Director, Editor

Attah Kojo has been farming cocoa for 30 to 40 years, and he has witnessed dramatic changes to his farms. He sees his yields declining and a proliferation of pests and fungus. He is skeptical that his farm will ever perform as it once had, and he fears that his entire community will suffer if this trend continues.

George Hozi's Story

Producer, Director, Editor

George Hozi is a young man who turned to cocoa farming because he couldn't find a job in his field. He recognizes the challenges that framing poses to his family's livelihood. In this video, he describes the trials and tribulations that come with farming cocoa in modern times.

James Tebi's Story
